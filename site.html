Story

Founded in August 21th of 1898 by a group of rowers, Vasco da Gama is the biggest club in the city of Rio de Janeiro. Known for being the club that always fought for social issues, Vasco displays proudly the nickname of Camisas Negras.
The Giant, as its called by all clubs, has the most prestigious story of brazilian football.
Know more about the most beautiful story in football at the official website.
